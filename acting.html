Acting to me is a way to escape to a another dimension. Joining a world that has been created and truly living in there as a different person. WOAH. It’s pretty weird to be honest haha, but I love it!

The first time I ever acted was on stage when my sister gave me the lead role in a church production as a demon. As I was on stage playing the role, I could see how frightened all the uncles, aunties and grandmas were, even when the play finished, they were giving me dirty looks haha! From that moment, I told myself, oh sh*t I must be pretty decent at this acting thing.

I then went onto doing GCSE Drama, then A-Level Drama, then Drama & Theatre Studies at university (which I bloody hated – too much unnecessary writing) and now I’m a “professional actor” whatever the hell that means.
