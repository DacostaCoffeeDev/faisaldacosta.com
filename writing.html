How the hell did I become a writer?! Hahahahahah! WTF.

I co-wrote a dramedy called I LOVE ACTING, BUT F*CK THIS INDUSTRY with Raphel Famotibe. It's a piece of art that will begin in the realms of theatre, then become a TV Series (God willing).

How did the idea of the play come about? Well, I was working in a school doing maintenance, refurbishing their library, whilst being covered in dust and slapping spiders off my arm. After a few days of this, a thought came to head – WHAT THE F*CK IS MY LIFE?

I've done all this actor training, got a degree, I've got a few decent TV & Film credits. This can't be life. I sat down, super depressed, as time went on the depression slowly became anger and I thought nahh f*ck this, I need to write this shit down.

I began to write down some really horrific thoughts and I called Raph and told him, yo I've got an idea, I want to write a play about the industry and I want to tell the truth. Pretty quickly Raph was on it.

During the writing of the play it developed from being called “The Acting Industry” to “I LOVE ACTING, BUT F*CK THIS INDUSTRY”. It became a play that was aiming to set EVERYONE free from the system and strong within who they are.

The play opened launched off at Theatre Peckham, selling out all three days we were there and will be coming back to life soon.

I dunno where my journey as a writer is going, but I can't lie, I love this sh*t.

Your sincere,

Faisal Dacosta
