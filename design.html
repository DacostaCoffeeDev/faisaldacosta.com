OWE NOTHING BUT LOVE

Owe nothing but love is a way of living. I try my best to follow these words as a guideline for the way I live my life. Really and truly, as human's beings, we don't owe anyone anything.

We're born alone and we die alone & death is the only thing that is guaranteed in this life. SO CARPIE DIEM! F*CK IT! Do whatever you want, be free, travel the world, make mistakes, take risks, be a rockstar, be whoever you want to be!

HOWEVER, whilst you're on this journey of owing nothing to anyone, you must love. Owe nothing but love.

The phrase ‘owe nothing but love' emerged from my brother hearing it at a sermon (Romans 13:8). Me and my beautiful woman design each piece and the goal is to make people feel free and comfortable. Wearing ONBL doesn't only spread the message to owe nothing but love, but it acts as a reminder to yourself.
